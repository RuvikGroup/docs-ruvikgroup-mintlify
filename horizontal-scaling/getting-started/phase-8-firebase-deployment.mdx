## Firebase Deployment Setup

### Step 18: Configure Firebase Hosting

- Update `.firebaserc` with your firebase project name
  ![Android Asset Links](/images/firebaserc.png)
- Update the site names in `firebase.json`
  ![Android Asset Links](/images/firebase-json.png)

**Important Notes:**

- Make sure to place your app signatures (SHA-1 or SHA-256) in Firebase project settings -> your android app -> `SHA certificate fingerprints`. You can find the signing keys inside `App Integrity` section in your app Google Play console.
