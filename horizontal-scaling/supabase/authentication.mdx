### Email/Password

- From you [project dashboard](https://supabase.com/dashboard), go to `Authentication` -> `Sign In / Providers`.

![Auth](/images/supabase/auth-tab.png)
![Auth](/images/supabase/sign-in-prov.png)

- Make sure you have `Allow new users to sign up` option enabled.
- Enable and configure the OAuth providers you want to support under `Auth Providers` (`Email` provider should be enabled by default).
- You will need to provide the `Client ID` and `Client Secret` from each provider.

### Apple

- Click on `Apple` under `Auth Providers`.

![Auth](/images/supabase/apple-auth.png)

- Fill the form with your Apple project [Client IDs](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/sign_in_with_apple/sign_in_with_apple_js) (e.g. com.example.app) and your [Secret Key](https://supabase.com/docs/guides/auth/social-login/auth-apple#generate-a-client_secret).

![Auth](/images/supabase/apple-auth-panel.png)

- Save aside the `Callback URL (for OAuth)`, it could be used later when using Sign in with Apple on the web in the Apple Developer Center.
- Click on `Save` at the bottom right of the panel.

### Google

- Click on `Google` under `Auth Providers`.

![Auth](/images/supabase/google-auth.png)

- Fill the form with your Google project Client IDs and your Secret Key. (Both can be found under `OAuth 2.0 Client IDs` in your project [Google Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.google.com/auth/clients))

![Auth](/images/supabase/google-auth-panel.png)

- Save aside the `Callback URL (for OAuth)`, it could be used later to register as callback URL when using Sign-in with Google on the web using OAuth.
- Click on `Save` at the bottom right of the panel.

### Facebook

- Click on `Facebook` under `Auth Providers`.

![Auth](/images/supabase/facebook-auth.png)

- Fill the form with your Facebook project client ID and your Facebook secret. (Both can be found in your project [Facebook Developer dashboard](https://developers.facebook.com/))

![Auth](/images/supabase/facebook-auth-panel.png)

- Click on `Save` at the bottom right of the panel.

### URL Configuration

- From you [project dashboard](https://supabase.com/dashboard), go to `Authentication` -> `URL Configuration`.

![Auth](/images/supabase/url-tab.png)

![Auth](/images/supabase/url-tab-prov.png)

- Set your `Site URL` to your production URL (e.g., `https://web.brand.com`).
- Add your development and staging URLs to `Redirect URLs` (e.g., `http://localhost:3000`, `https://webstaging.brand.com`).

### Email Templates

- From you [project dashboard](https://supabase.com/dashboard), go to `Authentication` -> `Emails`.
- Click on `SMTP Settings` tab to setup your SMTP email server details and be able to send emails.

> Get your SMTP details from your project email provider (e.g. [Resend](https://resend.com/settings/smtp))

![Auth](/images/supabase/emails-smtp.png)

- Click on `Templates` tab to setup your `Subject heading` and the `Message body` for each kind of email managed by Supabase.
- Customize the email templates to match your brand's voice and design.
- Click on `Save changes` for each template you customize.
